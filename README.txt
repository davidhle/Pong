 ------------
|INSTRUCTIONS|
 ------------
 -Use the up and down arrow keys to move the first/cyan player
 -Use the left and right arrow keys to move the second/green player
 -If a player scores a point, then there the scoreboard at the top will update according to the player's score
 -If a player wins, the game will end and the game will say who won.
